br.api.animas (anima, v)
Anima Powers functions. These functions are accessible via `br.player.animas.spell.functionName()`.

Parameters:

  • anima table The anima power table.
  • v number The ID of the anima power.
rank ()
Get the rank of a specific anima power.

Returns:

    number The rank of the anima power.

Usage:

    local anima = br.player.animas
 anima.spell.rank()  -- Gets the rank of the anima power, replace spell with name of spell to check.
